Body Mass Index (BMI) is a tool used to determine whether your weight is within a healthy range according to your height. Use this BMI calculator to monitor your health and take the right steps to achive ideal body balance. Enter your weight, height, and age in the columns provided, then click the 'Calculate BMI' button to see the results. The BMI calculation results will give you an idea of whether you are underweight, normal, overweight or obese.
